Understand

With the Directly platform, support leaders are able to access a company dashboard with access to each of the Directly APIs. Understand leverages a network of community experts and machine-learning to automatically classify customer issues into actionable intents and provide insight into the best next action to resolve them. Let Understand power the tedious but crucial job of accurately classifying all your customer issues.

Automate

With the Directly platform, support leaders are able to access a company dashboard with access to each of the Directly APIs. Automate leverages a network of community experts to scale the process of creating automated responses to resolve customer issues using machine-learning. Community experts provide continuous training to continually grow the number of issues resolved through automated responses, by creating the content and training behind automated responses. Start resolving more issues more quickly using Automate.

Engage

With the Directly platform, support leaders are able to access a company dashboard with access to each of the Directly APIs. Brands can use Understand to predict which action is best for resolving a customer problem. To enable this, contact center conversation data can now be uploaded, processed, and grouped together to create a map of clusters and prediction model. If an automation is predicted to be the best action, use Automate to help the customer in any supported channel with conversational AI. If an automated response fails, brands leverage Engage to recruit brand advocates with familiar product knowledge to help resolve issues that start in or move to digital channels at a fraction of the cost of the contact center. Users ask questions on their own time and an advocate will be there to help, with all the history of the conversation in one view.
